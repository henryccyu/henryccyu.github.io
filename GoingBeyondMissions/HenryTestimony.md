## 个人见证

I once thought I could achieve anything as long as I worked hard enough. I worked for major companies in China and Japan, and later joined Microsoft after moving to the United States. Everything seemed to be heading in the right direction.

However, I was under tremendous pressure working alongside so many talented people. My pride kept me from asking for help, so I tried to handle everything on my own. I worked extremely hard and became overwhelmed with stress.

One of my coworkers was very successful. Although he faced the same pressures I did, he was always calm and consistently delivered outstanding results. I wanted the kind of peace he had. Eventually, I learned that Jesus was the source of his peace and joy. That led me to accept Jesus as my Savior and Lord.

The burden I had carried for so many years was lifted. I no longer worried about what other people thought of me. My relationships with others improved, and I became more confident at work.

In 2009, I lost my job during the global financial crisis. Even after two months of searching, I still couldn't find another job. One evening, a brother from my church invited my family to his home for dinner. He prayed with me, reminded me of God's promises, and encouraged me to trust that God would provide the right job at the right time.

Soon afterward, I was offered a position in a field where I had no previous experience. Many other applicants were more qualified than I was, yet I received the job. From a human perspective, it made no sense. I knew it was God's faithful provision.

Following Jesus is the best decision I have ever made. My prayer is that you, too, will come to know His love and experience the hope, joy, and peace that only He can give.
